About me

Let's make something awesome together.

Ben Coldwell is a Graphic Designer and Front-End Web Developer in Kansas City, MO. He received a Commercial Art/Graphic Design Degree from CMSU (now UCM) and a Certificate of Completion for completing the Full-Stack Web Developer program at the KU Edwards Campus. He has over ten years of experience in advertising, building creative solutions to help brands grow.

When he’s not designing, he likes to spend his time watching the plethora of videos he’s purchased from Udemy, drinking coffee, watching local sports, spending time outdoors and trying to find his golf game that left him long, long ago. An all-around nice guy, if you'd like to work with him, please contact him here.
